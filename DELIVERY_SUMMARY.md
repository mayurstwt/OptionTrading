## 📈 Project Scope Summary

### What It Does
- ✅ Autonomous paper trading in Indian options (NIFTY/BANKNIFTY)
- ✅ Rule-based entry/exit (no manual intervention)
- ✅ Full risk management (position limits, daily loss limits)
- ✅ Complete P&L tracking and reporting
- ✅ Fully configurable (all rules in YAML, no hardcoding)
- ✅ Production-ready architecture

### What It Doesn't Do
- ❌ Real money trading (paper mode only, for now)
- ❌ Machine learning (rules-based only)
- ❌ Manual decisions (fully autonomous)
- ❌ Paid APIs (free only)
- ❌ Complex spreads with multi-leg Greeks (v1 handles simple spreads)

### Cost
- **APIs**: ₹0 (Upstox free tier)
- **Hosting**: ₹0-500/month (local or free-tier cloud)
- **Database**: ₹0 (SQLite)
- **Total**: **₹0/month** ✅

### Complexity
- **Architecture**: Intermediate (9 modules, clear interfaces)
- **Implementation**: ~2-4 weeks (full-time AI or developer)
- **Maintenance**: Low (well-documented, clear patterns)

### Skill Requirements
- Python 3.9+ or willing to learn
- Basic understanding of options trading
- Comfort with APIs and databases
- No advanced ML or quant skills needed

A: 3-4 weeks (AI + engineer), or ~2 months if doing it yourself

**Q: Do I need to know options trading?**  
A: Helpful but not required — you can learn by reading the code and papers

**Q: Can I use a different broker?**  
A: Yes — broker integration is abstracted (see MODULE 1 in MASTER_PROMPT.md)

**Q: What's the minimum setup?**  
A: Python + Upstox account + 30 minutes (see README.md Quick Start)

**Q: Can I go live?**  
A: Yes, but paper trade for 1-2 months first (Phase 8). Live trading in Phase 9.

**Q: Will it make money?**  
A: The framework is there, but strategy quality depends on your rules. Paper test first!

**Q: Is it secure?**  
A: Yes — credentials in .env (not in code), all trades logged, crash-safe database

**Q: Can it run on my laptop?**  
A: Yes, during market hours 9:15-15:30 IST

**Q: What if I break something?**  
A: Database persists all trades, you can always restore from backup
